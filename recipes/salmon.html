Pan-Seared Salmon

Description

Pan-seared salmon is a great way to cook up this fish. It’s easy, and the fillets are tender and full of flavor. The best part? The dish requires just a few ingredients—no need to check the recipe twice!

Ingredients

  • 4 (6 ounce) fillets salmon
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 2 tablespoons capers
  • ⅛ teaspoon salt
  • ⅛ teaspoon ground black pepper
  • 4 slices lemon
